Apple's Top Products

iPhone

The iPhone has been one the best phones since its release. The iPhone was released on June 29, 2007. After the first iPhone released Apple has then released iPhones 3 to iPhone 8, adding different models such as the S, SE and Plus. Apple then skipped the iPhone 9 and released the iPhone X and its models, the iPhone 11, and the iPhone 12. The latest model of the iPhone is the iPhone 13, which has a dual-camera system, an A15 processing chip, and supports a 5G network.

More Information: iPhone

Mac

Since the launch of the Mac in 1984, Mac has been the top choice of computers for many. When the Mac first released, it was a huge turning point for computers. After the release of the first Mac, Apple upgraded the Mac in innovative ways and released various portable and desktop computers. The latest desktop Mac is the M1 iMac, it has a 24 inch screen, a 4.5k display, and weighs less than 10 pounds. The latest portable Mac is the Macbook Pro, which features a Apple M1 chip, a 8-core GPU, and holds up to 20 hours of battery life.
